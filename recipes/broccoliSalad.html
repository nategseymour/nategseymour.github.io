Meg's Broccoli Salad

Ingredients
1 package broccoli cole slaw
1 can mandarin oranges, drained
1 package instant ramen noodles, crumbled (original/soy flavor, save seasoning packet for dressing)
2 bunches scallions, finely chopped
1 package sunflower seed kernels

Dressing ½ c rice vinegar
1 packet soy flavored seasoning from ramen package
¼ c vegetable or canola oil

A delicious and unique salad, great for summer and perfect for a pot luck.


Mix all the salad ingredients except ramen crumbles in a large bowl.
Whisk together the dressing ingredients separately and dress the salad just before serving.
Add the crumbled ramen last, or keep separate for leftovers since it will get soggy over time.

Enjoy!
